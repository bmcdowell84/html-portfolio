Brian's Favorite Horror Movies

My top 3 horror movies of all time

Halloween (1978)

It created the modern slasher we all love today.

Friday the 13th (1980)

Kicked off one of the best franchises of all time, and has a little twist for newbie watchers.

Scream (1996)

Another franchise favorite. It grounds slashers and shows it can happen anywhere, at any time.
